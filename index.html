Our Love Story

We came together and it became a rhapsody. And now her voice plays in my mind like a symphony

“Love is always patient and kind. It never jealous, love is never boastful or conceited.” Love is what I read in books, love is what I waited for and one fine day it arrived in my life too in a very unexpected way. And now when I connect the dots I reckon that the whole world had been conspiring behind me to get me the missing piece of the puzzle.

Like all other love stories our is also quite alluring since it commenced with acquaintance on social media which then metamorphosed into friendship when we worked on the same project of our college. We started spending time together in libraries, training centers and travelling. Those smirks and bickers, care and concern inadvertently reinforced our friendship.

As the day of academic severance came closer, the feeling of not being together crept inside our souls and the agony it caused deep inside our novice lovey-dovey hearts made us realize that “Love” has finally happened to us . And before our final adieu he came to me holding rose in his hands he went down on his knees. He proposed me for marriage and the entire class witnessed the same. Awestruck they were and soon the class was filled with the hubbub of applauses and good wishes.

And finally after 6 years of this beautiful journey called Love it’s time to start a new chapter in our lives by getting hitched. Thanks to our families for accepting us whole heartedly.
